Habitat: Grasslands and shrublands
Range:
  • The most social of all cats, lions keep in touch through roaring, which can be heard from up to five miles away.
  • The lion's signature mane is believed to help protect their throats in battle; males with the darkest, shaggiest manes are often the oldest and strongest. The manes, however, are not strictly exclusive to males; there have been numerous reports of female lions with manes and males without.
  • Lion populations have declined by about 50% in the previous century. Though scientists don't fully understand this rapid decline, much of the lion's natural habitat has been lost to agriculture, which ulitmately results in human-animal conflict.
