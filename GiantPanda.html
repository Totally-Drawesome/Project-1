Range:
  • Despite sharing the same name, giant pandas are not closely related to red pandas. The red panda is classified as a musteloid while the giant panda is a true bear.
  • Unlike most bear species, the giant panda is almost exclusively herbivorous, having even developed a special thumb-like wrist bone that allows for a better grip on bamboo shoots.
  • In 2016, the giant panda was reclassified from "endangered" to "vulnerable" on the IUCN Red List, after decades of conservation efforts. In spite of this, the overall population numbers in the low thousands, and the species is considered to be conservation reliant.
