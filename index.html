WORLD WILDLIFE



From the lush tropical rainforests of the Peruvian Amazon to the semiarid savannahs of East Africa, our planet is a haven of natural beauty and wonder. Dozens of unique ecosystems are scattered throughout the world, each one with its own unique variety of flora and fauna. Unfortuntely, many of these species have been threatened with extinction after years of human expansion throughout history. Now more than ever, it is important that we gain and sufficient understanding of the wildlife which share our planet. By learning more about these creatures, we can more effectively develop conservation programs to preserve our world's biodiversity for generations to come.
